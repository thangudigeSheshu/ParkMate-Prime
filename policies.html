Parking Rules
General Parking Rules
Last updated: Oct 12, 2025
Enforced Globally

1. Slot Adherence

Users must park strictly within the designated yellow lines of their booked slot number. Parking across multiple slots or in unassigned slots, especially Reserved or EV bays, will trigger an immediate system alert via overhead cameras.

2. Speed & Safety

The speed limit inside all ParkMate facilities is 10 km/h. Hazard lights must be turned on when reversing or finding your slot. Honking is strictly prohibited unless in an emergency.

3. EV Charging Bays

EV slots in Zone Q and R are exclusively for actively charging electric vehicles. Once your vehicle reaches 100% charge, you have a 30-minute grace period to unplug and move your car to a standard slot to avoid idle fees.

4. Overnight Parking

Overnight parking between 12:00 AM and 06:00 AM requires a specific overnight pass. Regular hourly tickets extending into this period automatically accrue an INR 80 flat overnight surcharge.
