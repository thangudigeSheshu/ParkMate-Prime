Trust Center & Policies

Comprehensive guidelines, rules, and privacy controls for ParkMate Prime.

Enforced Globally 24/7 Support: 1800-PARK-NOW
General Guidelines
  • Free First 15 Minutes
    Enjoy your first 15 minutes completely free. Dynamic billing starts only after this grace period ends.
  • Happy Hours Discount
    Park between 2:00 PM and 5:00 PM daily to receive an automatic 50% discount on standard hourly rates.
  • Speed & Safety
    Strict 10 km/h speed limit inside all parking zones. Hazard lights must be used when reversing or finding your slot.
  • Liability
    Park at your own risk. The facility is not liable for theft, loss, or damage to vehicles or belongings.
  • Account Security
    Only one active session allowed per account. Logging in on a new device will securely sign you out from the previous one.
Parking Grid Rules
  • Slot Adherence
    Vehicles must park strictly within the yellow lines of their assigned slot. Multi-slot occupation is tracked by ALPR cameras.
  • Oversized Vehicles
    SUVs, trucks, and oversized vehicles must use designated oversized zones (Zones P–Z).
  • EV Charging Bays
    24 EV bays (Zone Q & R) are exclusively for actively charging EVs. A 30-minute grace period is given after 100% charge before idle fines apply.
  • Prime Reserved Slots
    VIP and Rooftop slots are digitally locked and reserved exclusively for Prime members.
Tickets & Time
  • Arrival Grace Period
    Users must tap 'Arrived' upon reaching their slot. A 1-minute response prompt appears upon expiry, followed by a 15-minute grace time before auto-cancellation.
  • Cancellation Fees
    Cancellations made within 1 hour of booking incur a ₹10 charge. Cancellations after 1 hour incur a ₹75 charge.
  • Overnight Parking
    Parking between 12:00 AM and 06:00 AM requires an overnight pass, or it accrues a flat ₹80 overnight surcharge on top of standard rates.
  • Single-Exit Pass
    The generated QR Gate Pass is for single-exit use only and is non-transferable. Active SHA-256 hashing prevents tampering.
AES-256 Encrypted Payments
Wallet & Pricing
  • Dynamic Billing
    Billing is dynamically calculated every minute based on your vehicle type and zone. Deducted instantly from your ParkMate Wallet on exit.
  • Base Pricing Tiers
    Bikes: ₹15/hr | Cars: ₹25/hr | EVs: ₹30/hr | SUVs: ₹36/hr.
  • Prime Membership
    For ₹1999/month, members get automatic 20% cashback per trip, priority routing, and VIP zone access.
  • Pending Payment Lock
    If a user has an expired ticket with an unpaid balance, they are completely locked out of making new bookings until the deficit is paid.
ParkMind AI
  • PNAS Allocation System
    The Neural Allocation System scores slots out of 100 based on walking distance, zone entropy, vehicle size, and lift proximity for optimal parking.
  • AI Hold Locks
    AI-recommended slots are held temporarily in a locked state for 1 minute pending user confirmation to prevent double booking.
  • Anomaly Detection
    The AI monitors live revenue and transactions. Any spike resulting in a Z-Score > 2.5 triggers an immediate security alert in the Admin Command Center.

Privacy & Data
  • Minimal Collection
    We only store Name, Email, Vehicle Plate, Entry/Exit times, and Wallet Balance. Data is kept locally on your browser in pmStore.
  • Zero Third-Party Sharing
    No ad-tracking, behavioral profiling, or data broker sales. Period.
  • Data Retention
    Session data auto-expires after 24 hours of inactivity. Tickets are cleared upon exit. History is kept for 30 days.
  • Right to Erasure
    Users have the absolute right to erasure—you can instantly purge all your local data from the privacy settings or account menu.
